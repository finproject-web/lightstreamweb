Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you submit a loan application, we collect information necessary to process your request, including:

  • • Name, contact information, and address
  • • Financial information for eligibility assessment
  • • Employment and income details
  • • Banking information for fund disbursement

Technical Information: We automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website:

  • • IP address and browser type
  • • Device information and operating system
  • • Pages visited and time spent
  • • Cookies and similar technologies

2. Information Sharing

Verified Lenders Only: We share your application information exclusively with our network of verified lending partners. We do not sell your information to third parties.

Partner Network: Our lending partners are licensed financial institutions that have been thoroughly vetted and comply with applicable regulations.

Shared Information: We only share information necessary for loan processing and eligibility determination with potential lenders.

No Public Sharing: We never publish your personal information or make it publicly available without your explicit consent.

3. Security Measures

Data Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption to protect information in transit.

Secure Storage: Your information is stored on secure servers with limited access and regular security updates.

Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access your information, and all access is logged and monitored.

Compliance Monitoring: We maintain systems to detect and prevent unauthorized access or data breaches.

4. Your Rights

Access and Correction: You have the right to access, update, or correct your personal information at any time.

Data Portability: You can request a copy of your information in a readable format.

Opt-Out: You may opt out of marketing communications while maintaining access to essential services.

Complaint Process: We have established procedures for addressing privacy concerns and complaints.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Essential Cookies: We use cookies necessary for website functionality and security.

Analytics Cookies: We use analytics tools to understand website usage and improve our services.

Cookie Control: You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Policy Updates

Notification Process: We will notify users of significant privacy policy changes through website notices or email.

Effective Date: Changes become effective 30 days after notification unless required by law.

Continued Use: Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of updated policies.
