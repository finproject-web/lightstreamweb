Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loan services

You can apply for a personal loan by filling out our online application form. The process takes about 10-15 minutes and you'll need to provide personal information, employment details, and bank information.

We offer personal loans from $2,000 to $25,000. The exact amount you can borrow depends on your credit score, income, and other factors.

Our interest rates range from 7.99% to 15.99% APR. Your rate depends on your credit score, loan amount, and loan term. We offer competitive rates for qualified borrowers.

While we consider applicants with various credit scores, generally a credit score of 660 or higher will give you the best rates and terms. However, we have options for borrowers with lower credit scores as well.

Bank authentication helps us verify your identity and bank account information quickly and securely. This allows us to process your loan application faster and ensure funds are deposited to the correct account.

Most applications are approved within 1-2 business days. Once approved, funds are typically deposited into your bank account within 1 business days.

We offer flexible payment options including cash app or apple pay withdrawal, online payments, and phone payments or offline debit card payment from any local stores near by you . You can choose the payment method that works best for you.

Yes! We don't charge prepayment penalties. You can pay off your loan early at any time without any additional fees.
