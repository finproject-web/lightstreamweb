How It Works

Our simple process connects you with lending partners to find the best loan options for your needs.

1

Submit your application

Fill out our simple loan application form with your personal information, income details, and loan requirements. The process takes just a few minutes.

2

We evaluate eligibility

Our system reviews your application and matches you with lending partners that fit your profile and loan requirements. We use protected, encrypted processes.

3

We connect you with lenders

We forward your application to our network of trusted lending partners who will review your information and make lending decisions.

4

Lender completes review

The selected lender will complete their own review process and may contact you directly for additional information if needed.

5

You receive loan offer from lender

Once reviewed, you'll receive a loan offer directly from the lender with terms, rates, and next steps for funding.

Important Notice

LightStream is a loan facilitation service, not a direct lender. We connect borrowers with lending partners but do not make lending decisions ourselves.

Final loan terms, rates, and approval are determined by the individual lending partners.
