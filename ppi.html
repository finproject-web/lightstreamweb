What is Payment Protection Insurance?

Financial Protection: Payment Protection Insurance (PPI) is designed to help protect your loan repayments if you're unable to work due to illness, injury, or other qualifying circumstances.

Coverage Types: PPI typically covers:

  • • Accident and sickness coverage
  • • Unemployment protection
  • • Disability benefits
  • • Life insurance coverage

Voluntary Coverage: PPI is typically optional and can be added to or removed from your loan agreement based on your preferences and eligibility.

How PPI Works

Premium Payments: PPI premiums are typically added to your monthly loan payments and paid alongside your regular loan installments.

Claim Process: If you need to make a claim, you'll work directly with the insurance provider, not through our platform.

Cancellation Rights: You have the right to cancel PPI coverage at any time, with premiums adjusted accordingly.

Important Note: PPI is separate from your loan agreement and provided by licensed insurance partners. Review all terms carefully before accepting.
