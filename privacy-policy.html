Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Introduction

LightStream Financial Services ("LightStream," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our loan application and verification services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Address and demographic information
  • Employment and income details
  • Bank account information (account number, IFSC code)

Loan Application Information

  • Requested loan amount and purpose
  • Bank login credentials for verification purposes only
  • Credit and financial information for loan processing

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process and evaluate your loan application
  • To verify your identity and bank account ownership
  • To communicate with you about your application status
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To improve our services and website functionality

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure storage of personal information
  • Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with financial industry security standards

Important: We do not collect banking passwords, OTPs, or card numbers. Bank credentials are used only for account verification and are never stored.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements or court orders
  • With trusted service providers who assist in loan processing
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data (where legally permitted)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request information about data sharing practices

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze website usage, and provide personalized services. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.
