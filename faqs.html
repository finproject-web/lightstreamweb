Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loan facilitation services

You can apply for a personal loan by filling out our online application form. The process takes about 10-15 minutes and you'll need to provide personal information, employment details, and financial institution information. We connect users with lending partners. We do not provide loans directly.

Our lending partners offer personal loans from $2,000 to $25,000. The exact amount you can borrow depends on your credit profile, income, and other factors determined by the lending partners.

Interest rates from our lending partners typically range from 7.99% to 15.99% APR. Your rate depends on your credit profile, loan amount, and loan term as determined by the lending partners. We connect users with lending partners who offer competitive rates for qualified applicants.

While our lending partners consider applicants with various credit profiles, generally a credit score of 660 or higher will give you better rates and terms. However, our lending partners have options for applicants with lower credit scores as well. We connect users with lending partners. We do not provide loans directly.

Financial details help our lending partners verify your identity and account information quickly and efficiently. This allows lending partners to process your loan application faster and ensure funds are deposited to the correct account. We connect users with lending partners. We do not provide loans directly.

Most applications are reviewed by lending partners within 1-2 business days. Once reviewed and approved by a lending partner, funds are typically deposited into your account within 1 business day. We connect users with lending partners. We do not provide loans directly.

Our lending partners offer flexible payment options including various digital payment methods, online payments, and other convenient payment options. You can choose the payment method that works best for you through the lending partner. We connect users with lending partners. We do not provide loans directly.

Most lending partners don't charge prepayment penalties. You can typically pay off your loan early at any time without any additional fees, subject to the lending partner's specific terms.
