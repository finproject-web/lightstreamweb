LightStream Financial Services - Loan Terms & Conditions & Disclosure Policy

1. Introduction & Purpose

This document establishes the comprehensive policies, terms, and conditions governing the relationship between LightStream Financial Services and applicants/customers regarding Payment Protection Insurance (PPI), bank account verification processes, and loan transactions.

2. Payment Protection Insurance (PPI)

Based on risk assessment, credit profile, loan amount, and regulatory compliance requirements, certain applicants may be required to maintain Payment Protection Insurance as a condition of loan approval. PPI premiums typically range from $500 to $900, depending on loan amount, coverage level, term duration, and individual risk factors.

3. No Out-of-Pocket Cost Policy

LightStream Financial Services maintains a strict policy that customers are not required to pay Payment Protection Insurance premiums, verification fees, or compliance costs from personal funds. All required costs are structured within the loan framework.

4. Bank Account Verification & Negative Balance Policy

LightStream Financial Services strictly prohibits loan disbursement into bank accounts with negative balances, restrictions, freezes, or other limitations. When verification reveals account balance issues, the Company may issue verification credits to normalize account standing.

5. Restrictions on Use of Funds

Verification credits, PPI funds, and any restricted-use funds may not be withdrawn, transferred, or used for personal purposes. These funds are designated solely for specific, authorized purposes. Unauthorized use of restricted funds constitutes a material breach of these terms.

6. Customer Responsibilities & Representations

Customers must provide complete, accurate, and truthful information throughout the application process. Intentional misrepresentation or omission of material facts constitutes fraud and may result in application denial, legal action, or criminal prosecution.

7. Company Rights & Remedies

The Company reserves the right to reverse or cancel verification credits at any time for any reason, including suspected fraud, policy violations, or changes in verification requirements. The Company may terminate processing and withdraw any offer if policy violations are detected.

8. Misuse, Misrepresentation & Prohibited Conduct

Unauthorized use includes withdrawing verification funds for personal purposes, providing false information, using restricted funds for unauthorized purposes, or attempting to circumvent verification procedures. Misuse may result in immediate application denial, termination of existing loan relationships, reporting to law enforcement agencies, credit bureau reporting, and pursuit of legal remedies.

9. Legal Consequences

Customers agree to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless LightStream Financial Services from any and all claims, losses, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising from false information, misuse of funds, or violation of these terms and conditions. These policies are governed by applicable U.S. federal laws and the laws of the state where the Company operates.

10. IP Address & Location Tracking

For payment protection and fraud prevention purposes, your IP address and location data will be stored and monitored. In case of fraud, unauthorized fund diversion, or attempt to evade repayment obligations, this information may be used to locate you and assist law enforcement authorities in recovering funds and pursuing legal action.
